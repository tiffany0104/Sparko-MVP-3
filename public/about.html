Our Mission

We believe every startup begins with a spark—a simple idea that has the power to change the world.

Sparko's mission is to create a platform where entrepreneurs can meet the right co-founders, secure funding, and build lasting business partnerships. By blending intuitive design with smart matchmaking, we make networking easy and effective.

Our Story

Sparko began as a simple yet powerful idea from a single entrepreneur who struggled to find reliable co-founders and investors.

Traditional platforms like LinkedIn, while great for general networking, are not designed for fast, meaningful, or focused startup matchmaking—they often feel slow, impersonal, and inefficient.

Sparko is my answer to that challenge—a place where visionaries can connect with just a swipe, making collaboration simpler, faster, and more effective.
