Everything You Need to Know

Get quick answers to help you make the most of Sparko

What is Sparko?

Sparko is a matchmaking platform designed for entrepreneurs, investors, and co-founders. Think of it as 'Tinder for startups,' with additional tools like legal templates and premium services. Our platform connects like-minded individuals who are passionate about building successful businesses together.

How does the swipe-to-match feature work?

You swipe through potential partners or investors based on their profiles, business interests, and compatibility. When both parties swipe 'like,' it's a match, and you can start chatting. Our algorithm ensures you see the most relevant profiles first, increasing your chances of meaningful connections.

What are Super Sparks?

Super Sparks are highlighted matches that increase your chances of being noticed by potential partners or investors. Each user gets 3 free Super Sparks per week. When you use a Super Spark, your profile appears at the top of their stack with a special highlight, significantly boosting your visibility.

What legal templates are available?

Sparko offers templates like NDAs, Founder Agreements, and Term Sheets. These documents are professionally reviewed by legal experts and customized for startup needs. Our template library is constantly expanding to cover more legal scenarios that entrepreneurs commonly face.

Will there be other startup tools?

Yes! We are working on a Startup Tools Hub with Pitch Deck templates, financial modeling tools, and investor-ready checklists. Our roadmap includes market research tools, competitor analysis features, and integration with popular business software to create a comprehensive startup ecosystem.

How much does Sparko cost?

Basic matchmaking is free and includes profile creation, swiping, and basic messaging. Premium plans start from £9.99/month and include unlimited Super Sparks, advanced filters, and priority support. Legal templates are priced individually, starting from £29 per template.

Is my data secure?

Yes. We use industry-standard encryption and privacy protocols to keep your data safe. All sensitive information is encrypted both in transit and at rest. We comply with GDPR regulations and never share your personal information with third parties without your explicit consent.
