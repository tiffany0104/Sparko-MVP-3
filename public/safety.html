Your Safety Comes First

Sparko is committed to creating a trusted and secure environment for entrepreneurs, investors, and partners.

Our Safety Commitments

Verified Profiles

We encourage verified profiles to build trust and credibility between co-founders and investors.

Reporting & Blocking

You can report or block any user who behaves inappropriately, and our team will promptly review reports.

Data Protection

Your personal and business data is safeguarded with industry-standard encryption and privacy protocols.

Protecting Your Ideas

Your business ideas are unique and valuable. Never disclose sensitive details too early in the conversation.

Before discussing in-depth strategies or sharing confidential documents, make sure both parties sign legal agreements such as a Non-Disclosure Agreement (NDA).

Sparko provides templates for NDAs and other legal documents to help protect your intellectual property and future collaborations.

User Safety Tips

Validate Before Trusting

Research and verify the background of potential partners or investors before sharing detailed plans.

Avoid Oversharing

Do not reveal sensitive information, financial data, or trade secrets until you have established a secure and legal framework.

Use In-App Messaging

Start your conversations on Sparko's platform to maintain privacy and security.

Be Aware of Scams

Beware of anyone asking for money upfront or making unrealistic promises.
