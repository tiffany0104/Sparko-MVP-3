Our Commitment

At Sparko, we are committed to protecting your privacy and safeguarding your personal data. This Privacy Policy outlines how we collect, use, and protect your information when you use our platform.

1. Information We Collect

  • Account Information: Name, email address, password, and other details provided during registration.
  • Profile Information: Professional background, startup details, and any content you choose to share on your Sparko profile.
  • Usage Data: Information about your activity on Sparko, including matches, messages, and interactions.
  • Payment Information: If you purchase premium features or legal templates, we may collect payment details via secure payment processors.

2. How We Use Your Information

  • Provide and improve Sparko's matchmaking and communication services.
  • Process payments for premium features or legal templates.
  • Ensure a safe and trustworthy environment by detecting and preventing fraud.
  • Send you important updates, notifications, and promotional content (you can opt out at any time).

3. How We Protect Your Information

  • We use industry-standard encryption and secure servers to protect your data.
  • Access to personal data is restricted to authorized personnel only.
  • We regularly review our data protection practices to ensure compliance with applicable laws (e.g., GDPR).

4. Sharing of Information

  • We do not sell or trade your personal data.
  • We may share information with trusted third-party partners (e.g., payment providers) solely for the purpose of delivering our services.
  • We may disclose data if required by law or to protect the rights and safety of Sparko and its users.

5. Your Rights

  • You can access, update, or delete your account information at any time.
  • You have the right to request a copy of the personal data we hold about you.
  • You can withdraw your consent for marketing emails by using the "unsubscribe" link.

6. Contact Us
